Creator Community Rules

Please review before submitting

1. Kid-Friendly Content: All videos must be entirely safe for children (ages 4-12). No violence, inappropriate language, or adult themes.

2. No Ads or Placements: Direct advertising, sponsored messages, or external product links are strictly prohibited inside the video.

3. Educational Value: We prioritize content that teaches, inspires, or provides positive interactive value.

4. Human Review: Every video is manually reviewed. Violations will result in immediate creator account suspension.
